PartyPod is a subscription box for hosting parties. Each box includes a curated selection of themed products to help bring friends and families together for the next social gathering.

The brand was designed to project a simple, fun and creative voice while maintaining a sense of quality, where the geometric typeface Futura LT was selected for the logotype to enforce the visual identity. By incorporating bold typography and repetitive geometric patterns in a minimal manner, the brand is designed to appeal at not only adults but also towards a younger audience including teenagers and children.
