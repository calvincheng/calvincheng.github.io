Hi there — I’m Calvin Cheng, a recent graduate with interests in robotics, design and machine learning.

My engineering background along with my interest in design has led me to prioritise balancing function and aesthetics as the principle ethos behind my work. As such, I focus on strong layouts and typography in design, as well as ergonomic and intuitive engineering. Gravitating towards minimalism and geometry, I draw inspiration from nature, architecture and fashion. I have worked for various organisations such as Jaguar Land Rover and Bristol Robotics Laboratory. I’m a fan of cinema but a lover of music.
